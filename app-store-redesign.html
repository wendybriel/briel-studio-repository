PROCESS

A clear, calm process from first call to final assets.

1 — Discover

I review your current presence, product, and goals. We define what “success” looks like visually.

2 — Direction

I propose an aesthetic direction—palette, typography, and a signature motif aligned with your app.

3 — Design

I create a full screenshot suite, new icon concept, and revised copy that work together as a single story.

4 — Refine

We tune emphasis, copy, and sequencing until everything feels precise and on-brand.

5 — Deliver

You receive export-ready assets and simple guidance for App Store Connect implementation.

Optional — Ongoing

Seasonal variants, feature launches, and A/B concepts are available once the core system is in place.
